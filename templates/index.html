Quick Nutrition Tips

💧
Stay Hydrated

Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily. Start your morning with warm lemon water to kick-start metabolism.

🥗
Rainbow Plate

Eat vegetables of different colours daily. Each colour provides unique phytonutrients and antioxidants.

⏰
Meal Timing

Eat every 3–4 hours. Avoid skipping breakfast. Have your heaviest meal at lunch, lighter at dinner.

🌾
Whole Grains

Choose brown rice, whole wheat roti, oats, and millets over refined carbs for sustained energy.

🫘
Protein Every Meal

Include dal, paneer, curd, eggs, or legumes at every meal to support muscle health and satiety.

🚶
Move After Meals

A 10–15 minute walk after meals improves digestion and helps regulate blood sugar levels.
